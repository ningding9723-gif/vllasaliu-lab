Engineering Injection-Free Delivery of Biotherapeutics

We design nanomedicine and extracellular vesicle systems to overcome biological barriers and enable oral and mucosal delivery of peptides, proteins and RNA therapeutics.

Research Areas

Extracellular Vesicle Engineering
Understanding EV–cell interactions and developing hybrid systems for RNA delivery.

Nanomedicine & Lipid Platforms
Rational design of lipid nanoparticles and hybrid carriers for epithelial transport.

Injection-Free Biotherapeutics
Translating formulation science into non-invasive therapeutic strategies.

Advanced In Vitro Models
Organoid and co-culture systems for predictive drug delivery research.
