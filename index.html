Engineering Injection-Free Delivery of Biotherapeutics

The Vllasaliu Lab investigates biological barriers and develops nanomedicine and extracellular vesicle systems to enable oral and mucosal delivery of peptides, proteins and RNA therapeutics.

Research Themes

Extracellular Vesicles & RNA Delivery

Engineering extracellular vesicle and hybrid systems for oral nucleic acid and biologics delivery.

Nanomedicine & Lipid-Based Systems

Designing lipid nanoparticles and hybrid platforms to overcome epithelial and mucosal barriers.

Injection-Free Biotherapeutics

Enabling non-invasive administration of peptides, proteins and nucleic acids through rational drug delivery strategies.

Advanced In Vitro & Organoid Models

Developing predictive intestinal and organoid-based models to inform translational drug delivery research.
