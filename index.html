Biotherapeutics Delivery & Nanomedicine

The Vllasaliu Lab focuses on understanding and overcoming biological barriers to enable injection-free delivery of biotherapeutics. Our research integrates nanomedicine, extracellular vesicle engineering and advanced in vitro models to improve translational drug delivery strategies.

Research Focus

Extracellular Vesicle Engineering

Development of native and hybrid EV systems for nucleic acid and biologics delivery.

Lipid Nanoparticles & Hybrid Platforms

Rational design of lipid-based systems to enhance epithelial and mucosal transport.

Injection-Free Therapeutics

Enabling non-invasive administration of peptides, proteins and RNA therapeutics.

Advanced In Vitro Models

Co-culture and organoid-derived systems for predictive translational research.
